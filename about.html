Tree Elanza – A Premium Getaway

Nestled amidst the scenic hills of Kodaikanal, Tree Elanza offers a perfect blend of luxury and nature. As a unit of Santhi Ayurvedic Resort, we provide a peaceful retreat with breathtaking mountain views, well-appointed rooms, and top-notch amenities.

Our spacious accommodations feature modern furnishings, private balconies, TVs, electric kettles, and free toiletries. Enjoy a delightful dining experience with Indian, Chinese, seafood, and international cuisines, including halal and vegetarian options.

Relax in our spa and wellness center, unwind in our lush garden, or take in the views from our terrace. With free WiFi, ample parking, and a 24-hour front desk, we ensure a seamless and hassle-free stay.

Premium & Family-Friendly Accommodations

Located just a few kilometers from Kodaikanal’s top attractions, including Chettiar Park and Kodaikanal Lake, our retreat is ideal for couples, families, and solo travelers. Whether you seek adventure or relaxation, Tree Elanza promises an unforgettable experience.

With our attentive staff and exceptional hospitality, we guarantee a warm and memorable stay. Book your stay with us today and experience the best of Kodaikanal at Tree Elanza.
